      <div class="divider-line"></div>
    </div>
    <div class="case-study-text">
      <p><strong>Core objective:</strong> Establish the product's organic search advantage in global markets and build visibility across emerging AI search engines.</p>

      <div class="divider-line"></div>
    </div>
    <div class="case-study-text">
      <p><strong>Core objective:</strong> Build a high-calibre, in-depth content channel to attract global traffic through Gen Z topics and AI trend analysis.</p>
      <p><strong>Key actions:</strong> Produced in-depth reports on youth wellbeing, social safety, mental health, and AI trends while translating complex social psychology into intuitive, refined visual content.</p>
      <p><strong>Results:</strong> Generated over 1.06 million global impressions in a single month and reached a Google Search Console milestone of 17K clicks in 28 days, alongside strong dwell time and sustained SEO visibility.</p>

      <h3>3. High-Performance Cross-Border Acquisition Strategy &amp; Data Optimization</h3>
      <div class="divider-line"></div>
    </div>
    <div class="case-study-text">
      <p><strong>Core objective:</strong> Build a scalable paid acquisition system targeting iOS users in highly competitive markets such as the US and UK.</p>
      <p><strong>Key actions:</strong> Monitored audience distribution via Meta Ads backend data, identified the core converting audience, and used A/B testing plus creative testing to optimise CPI.</p>
      <p><strong>Results:</strong> Successfully maintained a low CPI across US and UK markets while using data reports to analyse acquisition costs by age group and gender for more sustainable efficiency.</p>
